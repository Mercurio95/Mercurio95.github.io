About me

I was born in Arezzo on 4 November 1995. I am currently a game design student.

My desire to become a game designer was born from the passion that i have towards playing in general. The part that i prefer most of my role as a game designer is that which goes from the concept phase to that of level design. I think that to create a good gaming experience two things are fundamental: the study of market trends (to try to understand what the users really want) and creativity. If simple mathematical calculations are enough to understand the first thing, it is not so easy to understand how to develop the second one. The creativity of a person is born from life experiences that makes and is potentially unlimited, but cannot be neglected (because it is not born from idleness) and that's why i constantly grow mine, trying to build the most diversified experiences, such as: playing different types of video games or board game, reading books, watching movies, visiting cities and cultural places. My dream is to give life to what my mind creates through the videogame.

Trip Tennis Tournament

School (Event Horizon Florence) project: "Trip Tennis Tournament" is a arcade-sports game, a variation of the classic tennis game in which the aim is to beat our opponent by hitting the walls behind him. To be able to complete the task, the player will have two shots available, the normal one and the loaded one. Another peculiarity of the game will be the different shape of the playing fields, in fact we will pass from the arenas that reflect the normal tennis courts, to the octagonal and circular arenas.

Concept idea: I give the input for the development of the initial idea and i contributed to the development of the core mechanic, the characters, the game arenas, the UI, the UX and the game balance.

Documentation: I took care of writing and updating the GDD and managing the rest of the documentation.

Team manager: I took care coordinate the work between the various work departments of the team (designer, concept artist and programmers).

Little Home

Global game jam project 2019: In this point and click adventure, the player will cross the journey of a homeless trying to survive the adversities of the world. The goal is to survive for a month, together with the character's dog. The message that the game wants to convey to the player is that the real home doesn't live within the same four walls, but can be found in a loved one; in this case, a dog. The gameplay is based on making choices.

Concept idea: Important role in the development of the initial concept, thinking the core mechanics, the game economy system and the level design of the only level.
